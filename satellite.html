01 LEO SATELLITE

EOS-01

A synthetic aperture radar (SAR) based all-weather Earth imaging satellite, EOS-01 was built by the Indian Space Research Organisation to support forestry, agricultural and disaster management operations.

Satellite type

RADAR IMAGINING

Launch date

7 Nov 2020

Purpose

Disaster Management System

02GEO SATELLITE

IRNSS-1I

IRNSS-1I is the eighth satellite in the Indian Regional Navigational Satellite System (IRNSS) series of satellites. The satellite's assembly, integration and testing is partly done by a consortium of six small firms led by Alpha Design Technologies, a Bengaluru-based aerospace firm under URSC's supervision.

Satellite type

Navigation

Launch date

12 Apr 2018

Purpose

Navigation System

03SSPO SATELLITE

SCATSAT-1

Scatterometer Satellite-1 is a satellite providing weather forecasting, cyclone prediction, and tracking services to India. It has been developed by ISRO Satellite Centre, Bangalore whereas its payload was developed by Space Applications Centre, Ahmedabad.

Satellite type

Earth Observation

Launch date

26 Sep 2016

Purpose

Climate And Environment Control

04GEO SATELLITE

INSAT

The Indian National Satellite System or INSAT, is a series of multipurpose geostationary satellites launched by ISRO to satisfy the telecommunications, broadcasting, meteorology, and search and rescue operations. It is a joint venture of the Department of Space, Department of Telecommunications, India Meteorological Department, All India Radio and Doordarshan.

Satellite type

Earth Orbitor

Launch date

10 Apr 1982

Purpose

Communication Satellite Systems
